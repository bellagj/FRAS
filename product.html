OUR GOAL AS A COMPANY

FRAS (Facial Recognition Attendance System) aims to revolutionize attendance tracking in educational institutions and workplaces. To achieve it, we provide the institutions an automated, efficient, and error-free solution using facial recognition technology. Our goal is to eliminate the inefficiencies of manual attendance-taking, enhance data accuracy, and improve time management and productivity.

TECHNOLOGY SPECIFICS

Simple, portable, and efficient.

FRAS is designed to be minimal and easy to implement. The main components in any type of service are:

  • GoPro HERO 12 Black: High-resolution camera to capture clear and precise images.
  • Tripod to ensure stability and an optimal angle for tracking.
  • Flash Disk (USB) containing the software and database, allowing offline and easy installation on any compatible computer.
